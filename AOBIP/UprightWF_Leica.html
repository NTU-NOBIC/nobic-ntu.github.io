| Upright Fluorescence Microscope - Leica DM5500



This motorised upright widefield fluorescence microscope features a fast and sensitive sCMOS camera for fluorescence detection and a colour camera for transmitted light. The available objectives cover a broad range of magnifications from 1.25x to 60x.

Available techniques:

  • Transmitted light imaging (brightfield, phase contrast, DIC)
  • Widefield multi-channel fluorescence imaging with a fast and sensitive camera

Objectives:

  • HCX PL Fluotar 1.25x/0.04 dry, FWD 3.7 mm, CG -
  • HCX PL Fluotar 5x/0.15 dry, FWD 13.7 mm, CG -
  • HC PL Apo 10x/0.4 dry, FWD 2.2 mm, CG 0.17 mm (Phase contrast)
  • HC PL Apo 20x/0.7 dry, FWD 0.59 mm, CG 0.17 mm (Phase contrast, DIC)
  • HCX PL Apo 40x/0.75 dry, FWD 0.28 mm, CG 0.17 mm (Phase contrast, DIC)
  • HC PL Apo 63x/1.4-0.6 oil, FWD 0.14 mm, CG 0.17 mm (DIC)
    [FWD = free workg distance, CG = cover glass]

Fluorescence excitation sources:

  • Metal-halide epifluorescence lamp

Filter sets:

  • DAPI (350/50; 400; 460/50)
  • FITC (470/40; 495; 525/50)
  • Cy3 (545/30; 570; 610/76)

Detectors and cameras:

  • Lumenera 5-5 CMOS grey-scale (2464x2056 pixels, 3.45 µm/pixel) with 0.55x C-mount adapter
  • DFC 450 C colour camera (2560x1920 pixels, 3.4 µm/pixel) with 0.7x C-mount adapter

Software:

  • LAS-X

Other features:

  • Motorised stage
  • Motorised filter turret
